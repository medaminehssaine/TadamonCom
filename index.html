How TadamonCom Works

Our platform changes disaster response from supply-driven to demand-driven, ensuring resources match actual needs.

1

Needs Registration

Communities register their specific needs categorized by type and urgency.

2

Resource Listing

Donors and suppliers list available resources they can provide.

3

Intelligent Matching

Our algorithm matches available resources to registered needs.

4

Coordinated Delivery

Relief organizations coordinate the efficient delivery of resources.
